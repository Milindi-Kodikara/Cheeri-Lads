About Rusu

For students,
by students.

RUSU is the only 100 percent independent body at RMIT. We are here to ensure your university experience extends beyond lectures and assignments. We are responsible for creating student life on campus. every week RUSU's purple colours will be out and about with free food and fun so make sure you check out our events.

RUSU Services

WE'RE HERE TO HELP

While RUSU is the heart of student life, RUSU is also here for when you need help.


COMPASS

Compass Student Drop in Centre provides free information, support and referrals to all RMIT students.

YOUR REPS

RUSU is made up of elected student representatives from all campuses to represent you.

VOLUNTEERS

Develop new skills and socialise with fellow students as you gain practical experience.

STUDENT RIGHTS

We put your rights first and provide independent advice and advocacy for all students.

Want more out of your uni or TAFE experience at RMIT? RUSU is here to help! We provide all the latest on RMIT clubs, events, student rights and giveaways.
